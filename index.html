Gain the opportunity to discover create build compete by participating in Explore Hacks

Explore Hacks is a 3 day hackathon that aims to grant both experienced and beginner student programmers the chance to explore the process of ideation to product execution. Participants also gain the opportunity to attend a variety of workshops and mini-events to enhance their programming skills and explore new areas in computer science. And it's all for free!

The prompt is to create a project that solves a current issue in the world and has an impact on the future.

FAQs

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is best described as an “invention marathon”. Anyone who has an interest in technology attends a hackathon to learn, build & share their creations over the course of a weekend in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere. You don’t have to be a programmer and you certainly don’t have to be majoring in Computer Science. - MLH

Why should I join Explore Hacks?

Explore Hacks is the perfect opportunity for you to learn something new no matter how much you already know while also having lots of fun through workshops, mini events and interacting with mentors. You will also gain the experience of how to use programming to solve real world problems.

How are projects judged?

Projects are judged based on 4 criterias: Feasibility, Impact, Uniqueness, and Creativity. When you submit your project, you will need to send us a 5 minute video that pitches the impact of your project along with a demo.

Am I eligible to participate?

Anyone who is a high school student ages 13+ or an undergraduate from the class of 2021 - 2026 is eligible to participate. The only prerequisite for Explore Hacks is a longing to learn! You do not need to know how to code to join!

Do I need a team to participate?

You may have teams of up to 4 people. You do not need to sign up with a team.

Do I need to pay to register?

Nope! Explore Hacks is completely free.

When and where is the hackathon?

The hackathon takes place from June 25, 2021 - June 27, 2021. The hackathon will be hosted completely online!

What if I want to use hardware for my project?

You can. But we won’t be able to provide you with any parts.

What do I do if I have a question, not on this list?

You can email us at support@explorehacks.org
